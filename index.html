Welcome to the official site of the MCCrusaders – Morris Catholic High School's FRC Team 7045. We compete in FIRST Robotics to push the limits of innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence.

About Our Team

Team 7045 is based out of Morris Catholic High School in Denville, New Jersey. Since 2018, we've been designing, building, and programming robots to compete at the highest levels of the FIRST Robotics Competition.
